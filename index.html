Chocolate Chip Cookies

  • Description
  • Ingredients
  • Instructions
  • Cooking Tips
  • Nutrition

Description

Classic chocolate chip cookies, baked fresh with rich butter and gooey chocolate chunks.

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Flour
  • Chocolate Chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven.
  2. Mix, butter, sugar, and egg together.
  3. Add the flour and chocolate chips.
  4. Scoop the dough.
  5. Bake.

Cooking Tips

  • Chill the dough 30 minutes before baking so cookies don't spread too thin.
  • Don't overmix once the flour goes in.
  • Pull cookies out when edges are golden but centers still look underdone.
  • Let them cool on the tray a few minutes before moving.
